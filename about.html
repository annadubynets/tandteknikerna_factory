About Tandteknikerna

Tandteknikerna Sverige AB Manufactures high quality products and services at low prices. The company specializes in crowns, bridges, all ceramic restorations, implants, and partial dentures. The dental technicians have helped thousands of dentists to restore oral health and smiles to their patients!
We start Tandtekinkerna Sverige AB with the task of maximizing this highly varied industry. With over 10 years of experience, we keep up to date with the latest developments to offer you the best and latest solution.
CAD / CAM technology now plays a very important role in helping us simplify, shorten and improve our process for you. The dental technicians aim to improve reliability, responsibility and improve both the physician and the patient’s experience. If you are ready to improve your patient care, call us to send your first case.
With this as a background and after careful examination, we started over 10 years ago our collaboration with a well-established laboratory in Shenzhen China which is certified according to ISO 9001: 2000, ISO 13485: 2003 and has the same objectives as us in Sweden. They are manufactured using the latest technology and high standard materials.

Quality Control of Work

Today, most of our production is done by our partners with a delivery time of between 10 and 14 days. But in order to be available to our customers in case of, for example, acute cases, we also offer a faster delivery time where manufacturing / adjustment takes place in Sweden.
It should be noted that before the jobs reach our customers, we perform quality control of the work to ensure the quality and other specifications such as color and design.
The dental technicians are registered with the Swedish Medicines Agency for the handling of specially adapted medical devices and comply with the regulations in LSVFS 2003: 11 on medical devices.
